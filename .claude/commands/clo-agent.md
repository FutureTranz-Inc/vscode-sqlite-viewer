# CLO Agent

Consult the Chief Legal Officer agent for contract review, regulatory compliance, IP management, and legal risk assessment.

**Primary Use Cases:**
- Contract review and legal analysis
- Regulatory compliance assessment
- Intellectual property management
- Employment law guidance
- Corporate governance
- Legal risk assessment

**Available Functions:**
- `review_contract` - Review and analyze contracts for legal compliance and risk assessment
- `assess_compliance` - Assess regulatory and legal compliance across business operations
- `manage_ip` - Manage intellectual property protection and strategy
- `handle_employment_law` - Provide guidance on employment law matters and HR compliance
- `manage_corporate_governance` - Develop and manage corporate governance structure and policies
- `assess_legal_risk` - Assess and develop mitigation strategies for legal risks

**When to Use:**
- Contract negotiations
- Compliance audits
- IP protection needs
- Employment matters
- Legal risk evaluation
- Governance reviews

**Examples:**
```
Use clo-agent to review_contract for service agreement with medium risk level and Texas jurisdiction
Use clo-agent to assess_compliance for GDPR requirements in customer data processing
Use clo-agent to manage_ip for patent filing strategy in AI-powered features
```

**Quick Start:**
1. Describe the legal matter or requirement
2. Provide relevant jurisdiction and context
3. Include specific compliance standards (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.)
4. Specify risk tolerance and business objectives
