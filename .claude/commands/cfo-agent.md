# CFO Agent

Consult the Chief Financial Officer agent for financial analysis, ROI calculations, budget planning, and business decisions.

**Primary Use Cases:**
- Financial projections and ROI analysis
- Budget management and allocation
- Business plan development
- Cash flow tracking and optimization
- Financial analysis and benchmarking

**Available Functions:**
- `create_business_plan` - Generate comprehensive business plans with executive summary and financial projections
- `generate_financial_projections` - Create P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow forecasts
- `analyze_financials` - Perform financial analysis with ratios, trends, and benchmarking
- `manage_budget` - Create and manage budgets with departmental allocations
- `calculate_roi` - Calculate ROI with NPV, IRR, and risk analysis
- `track_cash_flow` - Monitor cash flow with forecasting and optimization

**When to Use:**
- Building business cases for features
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Resource budget planning
- Investment decisions
- Financial health assessment

**Examples:**
```
Use cfo-agent to calculate_roi for the customer portal project with $250K investment over 24 months
Use cfo-agent to generate_financial_projections for Q1 2025 with conservative growth assumptions
Use cfo-agent to manage_budget for development team expansion with $500K annual allocation
```

**Quick Start:**
1. Describe your financial analysis need
2. Specify the scope (project, department, company-wide)
3. Include relevant financial data or assumptions
4. Request specific analysis type (ROI, projections, budgeting)
