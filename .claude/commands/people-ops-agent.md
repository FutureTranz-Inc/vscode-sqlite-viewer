# People Ops Agent

Consult the People Operations agent for talent acquisition, employee development, performance management, and culture initiatives.

**Primary Use Cases:**
- Talent acquisition and recruiting
- Employee onboarding and development
- Performance management
- Compensation and benefits
- Employee relations
- Culture and engagement

**When to Use:**
- Hiring initiatives
- Performance reviews
- Compensation planning
- Employee development
- Team culture initiatives

**Examples:**
```
Use people-ops-agent to develop hiring strategy for senior developer positions
Use people-ops-agent to create performance management framework for remote teams
Use people-ops-agent to design compensation structure for startup scaling phase
```

**Quick Start:**
1. Describe the HR challenge or initiative
2. Provide organizational context and size
3. Include industry benchmarks or constraints
4. Specify desired outcomes and timelines
