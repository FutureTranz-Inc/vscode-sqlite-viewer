# CMO Agent

Consult the Chief Marketing Officer agent for marketing strategy, brand management, campaign planning, and brand compliance validation.

**Primary Use Cases:**
- Marketing strategy and campaign planning
- Brand management and positioning
- Content strategy development
- Marketing performance analysis
- Brand compliance validation

**Available Functions:**
- `create_marketing_strategy` - Develop comprehensive marketing strategy with goals, tactics, and metrics
- `analyze_brand_positioning` - Assess current brand position and recommend improvements
- `plan_content_strategy` - Create comprehensive content marketing strategy and calendar
- `analyze_marketing_performance` - Evaluate marketing campaign performance and ROI
- `validate_brand_compliance` - Validate content against brand voice, messaging, and style guidelines

**When to Use:**
- Planning marketing campaigns
- Brand development initiatives
- Content strategy planning
- Campaign performance reviews
- Brand consistency checks

**Examples:**
```
Use cmo-agent to validate_brand_compliance for customer email campaign against FutureTranz brand guidelines
Use cmo-agent to create_marketing_strategy for product launch with $50K budget and 3-month timeline
Use cmo-agent to analyze_brand_positioning for enterprise software targeting healthcare industry
```

**Quick Start:**
1. Describe the marketing objective or challenge
2. Provide brand context and target audience
3. Include budget and timeline constraints
4. Specify campaign goals and KPIs
