# Sales & Marketing Agent

Consult the Sales & Marketing agent for pipeline management, campaign development, lead analysis, and conversion optimization.

**Primary Use Cases:**
- Lead management and qualification
- Pipeline analysis and optimization
- Marketing campaign development
- Conversion tracking and analysis
- Lead scoring and nurturing

**Available Functions:**
- `manage_pipeline` - Analyze sales pipeline with stage breakdown and conversion rates
- `generate_campaign` - Create marketing campaign strategy with budget allocation
- `analyze_leads` - Assess lead quality with scoring and source performance
- `track_conversions` - Track and analyze conversion rates across funnel stages

**When to Use:**
- Pipeline reviews
- Campaign planning
- Lead qualification
- Conversion optimization
- Marketing ROI analysis

**Examples:**
```
Use sales-marketing agent to manage_pipeline for Q1 2025 with conversion goals and bottleneck analysis
Use sales-marketing agent to generate_campaign for product launch with $25K budget and email focus
Use sales-marketing agent to analyze_leads for webinar registration quality scoring
```

**Quick Start:**
1. Describe the sales or marketing objective
2. Provide current pipeline or campaign data
3. Include target metrics and budget
4. Specify target audience and channels
