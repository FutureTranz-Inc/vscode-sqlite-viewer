# CRO Agent

Consult the Chief Revenue Officer agent for revenue strategy, growth planning, sales process optimization, and customer success programs.

**Primary Use Cases:**
- Revenue strategy and growth planning
- Sales process optimization
- Customer success programs
- Growth metrics analysis
- Pipeline management

**Available Functions:**
- `create_revenue_strategy` - Develop comprehensive revenue strategy and growth plan
- `analyze_growth_metrics` - Analyze growth metrics and identify optimization opportunities
- `optimize_sales_process` - Optimize sales processes and team performance
- `develop_customer_success` - Develop customer success and retention strategies

**When to Use:**
- Revenue growth planning
- Sales process improvements
- Customer retention initiatives
- Growth opportunity analysis
- Sales team optimization

**Examples:**
```
Use cro-agent to optimize_sales_process for enterprise sales team with 45-day average cycle
Use cro-agent to analyze_growth_metrics for SaaS product with 15% monthly growth target
Use cro-agent to create_revenue_strategy for market expansion into healthcare vertical
```

**Quick Start:**
1. Describe the revenue challenge or opportunity
2. Provide current sales metrics and processes
3. Include market context and competition
4. Specify growth targets and timelines
